Overview of What We Do

Consumer spending
Which products and services are US consumers buying, and where? Data includes expenditures by households for 20+ categories including apparel, food and beverage, and financial. Data Sources: Esri, Consumer Expenditure Surveys, and Bureau of Labor Statistics

Market Potential data
Data on products and services consumers use and demand. View the expected number of consumers and Market Potential Index data for goods, services, attitudes, and activities. Data Sources: Esri and GfK MRI

Business Locations and summary data
Explore data on more than 13 million businesses, including name and location, industrial classification code, number of employees, and sales. Data Sources: Esri, Infogroup, and SafeGraph

Customer Tapestry Segmetation
Detailed market segmentation of US residential neighborhoods divided into 67 segments based on socioeconomic and demographic characteristics. This data provides insights into customer lifestyle and behaviors. Data Source: Esri
